WE ARE
CLERKS

Founded in 1825, Clerks has been
a leader in footwear design and
craftsmanship for nearly two
centuries. From humble beginnings
in Street, Somerset, our family-owned
company has grown into a global brand
renowned for quality, comfort, and
innovation.
